Operation Run

SPLASH IMAGE / ICON

Operation run is a school project that I made with a team of 4 others. The project spanned two months.

The Concept for the game is that when you kill an enemy, you get their unique weapon, and must use each weapon to collect 4 gems and finish each level as fast as possible.

RESPONSIBILITES

I Acted as project lead and lead programmer on Operation Run, and was responsible for creating all 4 weapons, enemy attacks, and the saving and loading system.

Asside from this, I also assisted the UI programmer, Created Particle Effects, Shaders, and Animations where free assets could not be used, and created the Level Specific mechanics/objects such as falling platforms, moving walls, breakable doors, and boss mechanics.
